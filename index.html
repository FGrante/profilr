Welcome on Profilr!

Profilr helps you to find thousands of profiles on social networks corresponding to your criteria. Complete your criteria, choose the social network you want to search in and Profilr generates an advanced search on Google to list all the profiles.

Examples:

  • Ruby developers in San Francisco on Linkedin
  • People working at Google on Google+
  • Designers in Berlin on Dribbble
  • LinkedIn
  • Google+
  • Viadeo
  • Twitter
  • Github
  • Dribbble
Advanced options
  • Any time
  • Past 24 hours
  • Past week
  • Past month
